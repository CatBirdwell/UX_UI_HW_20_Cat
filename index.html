Problem Statement

Most platforms regarding wellness alternatives are crowd-sourced, making it difficult to trust the ‘facts’ and opinions provided. Young adults need a way to search for verified, expert-supported information on wellness alternatives. We propose an app that acts as an interactive informational index of herbs and supplements sourced from experts to be the solution.
